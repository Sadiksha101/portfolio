WEAVOLUTION

Weavolution was created for a client of Alternative Technology Pvt. Ltd. and involved designing and developing a form for ordering a copy of the Weavolution book. After users filled out the form with their details, an email was automatically sent to the provided email address with order confirmation and other relevant information. I was responsible for both the UI/UX design and the development of this project, ensuring the form was user-friendly, functional, and visually appealing. The project streamlined the order process and provided users with a seamless experience from submission to receiving the confirmation email.
