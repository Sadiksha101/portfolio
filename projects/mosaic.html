Mosaic Me

Mosaic Me allows users to upload an image, which is then transformed into a mosaic using the intricate designs of rugs and carpets. The user-uploaded image is broken down into smaller sections, each represented by a rug or carpet design, creating a unique and artistic representation of the original image. I handled both the UI/UX design and the development, ensuring a seamless user journey and high-quality results for each mosaic creation.
