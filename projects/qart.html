QaRt

Qart is an innovative web application developed by Alternative Technology Pvt. Ltd. that enables users to generate QR codes for any data or website and seamlessly integrate them onto custom images. My role in this project was to design the UI/UX, ensuring an intuitive, user-friendly experience. I focused on creating a clean, modern design with easy-to-use navigation, allowing users to quickly upload their desired image, input their URL or data, and generate a QR code with a seamless overlay on the image.
