Ramro Designs

Ramro Designs (/rāmrō/) , meaning "Beautiful Designs" in my native language Nepali, is an innovative platform created for the rug, carpet, and textile industry. It serves as a hub for inspiring design collections, providing valuable resources for both established professionals and aspiring designers.

I contributed significantly to the design and user experience. I crafted the About Us page to showcase the brand's story and values through a visually appealing and engaging interface, ensuring it resonated with the target audience.

Additionally, I designed the invoice sent to users after their purchase, focusing on clarity, professionalism, and alignment with the brand’s identity. I also created the pamphlet for the Black Friday coupon claim, which was distributed via email, ensuring it was both eye-catching and informative to maximize user engagement during the promotional period.

Only1DollarDesign & OnlyHundred

For both the Only1DollarDesign and OnlyHundred websites, I was responsible for designing the UI/UX of the About Us page. I focused on creating an engaging and user-friendly layout that clearly communicated the mission, values, and story behind each brand. The design aimed to build trust and connection with visitors, ensuring they understood the vision of the websites while maintaining a cohesive visual style that aligned with the overall branding. My approach prioritized ease of navigation, clean design, and a clear presentation of key information.

Personal Projects

Here are some of the projects that I have worked on to explore and enhance my skills in design and development.

Just Bakes

Just Bakes is a conceptual bakery app designed to bring the charm of a cozy bakery into the digital world. The app offers a seamless user experience for browsing and purchasing freshly baked goods, including donuts, cakes, pancakes, and cupcakes. Users can explore products through streamlined categories and personalized recommendations. The design emphasizes ease of navigation, aesthetic appeal, and functionality, ensuring users can browse and purchase their favorite items effortlessly.

Delish Coco

Delish Coco is a conceptual website crafted for a chocolate factory tour. This self-initiated project was undertaken to practice and enhance my UI design skills. The website provides a visually rich and engaging experience, immersing users in the journey of chocolate making. This project allowed me to experiment with layout design, color theory, and user interaction elements while maintaining a cohesive and appealing visual style.
