Ramro Designs

Ramro Designs (/rāmrō/), meaning "Beautiful Designs" in my native language Nepali, is an innovative platform created for the rug, carpet, and textile industry. It serves as a hub for inspiring design collections, providing valuable resources for both established professionals and aspiring designers. Alongside the unique designs, customers also receive the associated color-separated digital artwork and a certificate entitling them to use the designs. In collaboration with a colleague, I contributed to building the frontend of this platform for Alternative Technology Pvt. Ltd.
